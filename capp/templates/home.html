EcoTracker

Measure. Reduce. Contribute.

Hello, and welcome to our carbon emissions calculator app! Our mission is to empower individuals and companies to understand their carbon footprint better and make informed steps toward reducing their environmental impact. Our app is user-friendly and offers customized advice on how to reduce emissions tailored to your personal habits or company operations. With our app, you can monitor your carbon emissions and implement significant changes to lessen your effect on our planet while contributing to a more sustainable future. Join us in the effort to protect our Earth today!

This semester, our cohort of economics students has taken the Green Digitalization and App Development course. Under the guidance of our instructor, Mario Blázquez de Paz, we've been working collectively to create and launch our own applications as part of a team project. We've utilized a range of programming languages such as HTML, CSS, Flask, Latex, Python, and JavaScript to architect our apps. Our learning journey has included mastering the deployment of these applications on platforms like Amazon Web Services, and our collaborative efforts have been coordinated through Github. Should you have any questions about the application or wish to learn more about its developers, please feel free to contact our group or reach out to individual members via their LinkedIn profiles.
